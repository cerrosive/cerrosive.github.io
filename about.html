my motivations

Game development encapsulates all of my interests in one, that being art, design, coding, music, etc. I've always been a very creative person, finding enjoyment in many aspects of creation, however I didn't want to confine myself to just one. That's why I got into game development.

My household is incredibly creative. Being raised by composers and raised to allow my art to cultivate has allowed me to open my eyes to a whole world of creation. It's through this that I've found enjoyment and intuitive skill in music, art, coding, etc. I have been an online artist creating fanart and selling commissions for many years now, along with taking many classes to take my skills even further, I've written and composed original music/songs, I got into coding from quite early on, learning both on my own and attending classes and summer camps for it.

But more than anything, I love worldbuilding. I find great enjoyment in creating these complex stories and building the world around them. I've created entire multiverse systems with it's own laws and deities and universes. I've created whole countries with their own cultures, conlangs and history. Through game development, I'm given the opportunity to bring the worlds I have created to life, and share them with everyone else to experience and enjoy. Sharing my stories is my ultimate goal.
